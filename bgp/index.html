bgp

This page is about AS57048 and how to peer with me. AS57048 is an ipv6-only hobby-ASN with no other purpose than to learn as well as to provide me with ipv6 connectivity in places where I don’t have it.

General info

AS57048 runs on a VM on a proxmox server co-located at coloclue in Amsterdam. I run a mix of physical interconnects as well as tunneled connections for transit and peering.

General information on AS57048 can be found in a couple of places: peeringdb bgp.tools bgp.he.net

I announce only a single prefix ( 2a0e:f7c0::/29 ). I do strict RPKI ROV validation.
